
========================================================================================
WHAT THE TERM "BINARY" CAN MEAN 
========================================================================================

Depends on the context : 

  (a) an app's executable

  (b) raw binary    [meaning]    raw binary data    [is]    binary representation of a number (int, float, or double)

  (c) pure binary = raw binary

  (d) flat binary = binary code and data that a computer can understand 
     . there are no headers or metadata of any kind
