The "ILoveYou" attack, a.k.a. Love Bug:
 . launched on May 4, 2000
 . a malicious executable emailed as a "love letter" text file
 . spread to millions of computers with Windows on them
 . attached itself to and spread through files and folders
 . discovered more email addresses on target machines and would send itself to those targets
 . would launch various sorts of malicious software
 . would replace files
 . would hide itself after it did the deed
 . across the globe
 . billions of dollars in damages
