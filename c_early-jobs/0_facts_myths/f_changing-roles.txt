
IT Consultant:
 . "An IT consultant analyzes a client's technical needs and then recommends, designs, or implements technology
    solutions to improve their systems, efficiency, and business outcomes."


PC Provisioning Technician:
 . "A PC provisioning technician prepares, configures, and deploys computers so they’re ready for end-users with
    the correct software, settings, and security policies."


Site Reliability Engineer:
 . "A site reliability engineer builds and maintains automated systems that keep large software services reliable,
    fast, and scalable."


Network Engineer:
 . "A network engineer designs, configures, and maintains the hardware and protocols that allow computers and devices
    to communicate securely and efficiently."


Security Engineer:
 . "A security engineer designs, builds, and operates systems that protect Google’s infrastructure, users, and data
    from security threats at massive scale."


IT Support Specialist:
 . "An IT support specialist helps users troubleshoot, resolve, and prevent technical problems with computers,
    software, and networks."


Embedded System Engineer vs. Embedded System Developer:
 . "An embedded systems engineer typically focuses on designing and integrating hardware–software systems at a system
    level, while an embedded systems developer is more focused on writing and maintaining the firmware and low-level
    software that runs on that hardware."


Sales Engineer:
 . "A sales engineer explains and demonstrates complex technical products to customers and helps design solutions
    that meet their needs to support the sales process."
