
Firefox :

 . Ctrl + T		= create new tab
 . Ctrl + W		= close currently active tab.		The tab will be remembered , i.e., pushed onto the stack of closed tabs.
 . Ctrl + Shift + T	= reopen the most recently closed tab. 	The tab will be forgotten  , i.e., popped off the stack of closed tabs. 
 . Ctrl + L		= select URL text field
 
 . Ctrl + left		= page to prev track of YouTube playlist
 . Ctrl + right		= page to next track of YouTube playlist
