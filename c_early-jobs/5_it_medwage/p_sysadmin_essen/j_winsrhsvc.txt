
===================================================================================
WINDOWS SEARCH SERVICE
===================================================================================

“The Windows Search service is a background service that indexes files, emails, and other content on a Windows
 system to provide fast and efficient search results.”


The index:
 . "the database that stores the cataloged information about files and their properties for quick retrieval
    during searches"


The indexer:
 . "the component that scans and catalogs file names, contents, and properties to enable fast search results"
