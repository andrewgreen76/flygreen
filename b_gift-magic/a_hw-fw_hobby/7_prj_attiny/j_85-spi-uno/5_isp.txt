
===================================================================================
WHAT IS ISP ? 
===================================================================================
"
In embedded engineering, **ISP (In-System Programming)** refers to the ability to program a microcontroller or other programmable device directly on the circuit board, without needing to remove it, typically via interfaces like **SPI, JTAG, or UART**.
"

===================================================================================
WHAT ARE MOSI vs. MISO ? 
===================================================================================
"
... **MOSI (Master Out Slave In) and MISO (Master In Slave Out)** are **digital signals** used for data transfer in the **SPI (Serial Peripheral Interface) protocol**.
"
