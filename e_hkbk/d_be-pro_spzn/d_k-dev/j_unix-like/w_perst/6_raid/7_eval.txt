=================================================================================
WHAT GOES ON RAID FIRST :
=================================================================================

 . metadata
 . parity information
 . ECC
 . boot sector
 . system files
 . hidden files
 ...

Then goes user data. 

=================================================================================
RAID EVALUATION CRITERIA :
=================================================================================

Raw capacity : 
 . total size of RAID (occupied and unoccupied blocks)
 . first occupied with RAID configuration 
 |
 . N drives
 . B blocks per drive 

Usable capacity : 
 . leftover space for user data (logical volume)
