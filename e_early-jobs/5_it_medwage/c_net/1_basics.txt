MISCELLANEOUS QUESTIONS 
===================================================================================

Q : What does it mean for a user to be unable to connect to the internal system ?
A : "It means the user cannot establish access—due to network, authentication, or configuration issues—to
     the organization’s private systems or resources."


Q : What qualifies as an administrative change in networking?
A : "An administrative change in networking is any intentional configuration modification made by an administrator,
     such as updating IP addresses, routing tables, firewall rules, or access controls."
