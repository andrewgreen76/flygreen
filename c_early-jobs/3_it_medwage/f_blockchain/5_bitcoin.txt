"
Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that enables peer-to-peer transactions on a blockchain without
  a central authority.
"


"
Bitcoin was introduced in 2009 by an individual or group using the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto.
"


"
Mining Bitcoin requires a machine to perform some computation that takes up your machine's resources. At the end,
  you may be rewarded with some amount of Bitcoin. 
"
