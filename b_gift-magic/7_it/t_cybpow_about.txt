
===================================================================================
ABOUT : CyberPower PC model SLC7000BST
===================================================================================

"
Gigabyte B450M DS3H WiFi is the motherboard model used in the CyberPowerPC SLC7000BST gaming desktop, as confirmed by customer specifications for the AMD Ryzen 7 3700X configuration sold at Best Buy.
"

"
The CyberPowerPC SLC7000BST gaming desktop features a PCIe 3.0 generation on its Gigabyte B450M DS3H motherboard, supporting up to PCIe 4.0 on the primary GPU slot with a BIOS update and compatible Ryzen 3000-series CPU, though it defaults to PCIe 3.0 for broader compatibility. 
"

The AMD Ryzen 7 3700X :
 . desktop processor
 . 8-core, 16-thread
 . from AMD's 3rd-generation Ryzen series
 . built on a Zen 2 architecture (7nm)
 . high performance for gaming, content creation, multitasking
 . base clock = 3.6 GHz
 . boost clock = up to 4.4 GHz
