
REMINDERS :
_________________________________________________________________________________________

Segmentation fault :
. illegal access (access to uninitialized or free memory) 

Page fault : an event of either ... 
. accessing a virtual page and attempting to look up a non-existing corresponding frame 
. attempting to access a page that was swapped out to the disk 

Protection fault - a generic term for all of the following : 
. accessing a page with insufficient permissions
. seg.fault 
. a page fault

.
