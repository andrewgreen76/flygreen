==================================================================================================================

 . introduced in 2008
 . with ext4 
 . still used in various modern file systems  


Logging :
  |
  . [file-ops] ---> journal 
 -> [metadata] ---> journal
 -> [  data  ] ---> journal 


Crash -> recovery :
 . involves reconciliation of metadata and data
