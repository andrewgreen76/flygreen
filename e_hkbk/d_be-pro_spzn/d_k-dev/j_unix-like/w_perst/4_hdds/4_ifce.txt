===================================================================================
HDD INTERFACE 
===================================================================================

Host :
 . the computer HDD is connected to

HDD interface :
 . a physical/electrical connection 
 . host RAM <> HDD cache

Sector = block :
 . fixed-size unit of data

HDD :
 + several sectors 
    . sector = 512 bytes
    . address space : 0 -> n-1 
 + a dedicated cache

===================================================================================

 . a.k.a. PATA
