
API (application programming interface, or simply interface) :
 . structures and functions implemented to aid in the development of something at a higher , more abstract level
    . they may belong to a system (system calls) or a library (library functions) 


Platform :
 . a system
 . OS , environment , or hardware architecture 


Cross-platform : 
 . works across multiple platforms (esp. OS's) 
 . ("without requiring modification or recompilation")
