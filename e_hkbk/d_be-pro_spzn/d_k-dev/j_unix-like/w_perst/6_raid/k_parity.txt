
Parity :
 . a method of enforcing data integrity across RAID


Parity bits :
 . used in error detection 
 . parity bits accompany corresponding data 
    . error-checking : comparing parity bits 
 . may need a parity disk 

================================================================================
 . ONES = sum of 1's in data
 . EVEN_SUM = even number of 1's in data 
 . ODD_SUM = odd number of 1's in data 
 . (ONES + 1) % 2 = parity bit
 
================================================================================
Odd parity arrangement :
 | 
 . (EVEN_SUM + [parity_bit] ) % 2 = odd (1)		= parity bit 
 . (ODD_SUM + [parity_bit] ) % 2 = even (0)		= parity bit 

================================================================================
Even parity arrangement :
 | 
 . (EVEN_SUM + [parity_bit] ) % 2 = odd (1)		= parity bit 
 . (ODD_SUM + [parity_bit] ) % 2 = even (0)		= parity bit 

================================================================================


Parity checks :
 . error-checking
 . fault tolerance
 . reconstruction after disk failure

    . calculated parity <- from the RAID controller or the host's CPU
