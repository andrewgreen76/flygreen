
Multiplexing traffic : 
 . a sender node DIRECTS PACKETS to services on other nodes 


Demultiplexing traffic : 
 . the recipient node TAKES INCOMING PACKETS to specific processes 


Thanks to multiplexing and demultiplexing one server machine can run all the applications for a small business.
 . website content and functionality, email servicing, printing services, etc.
 . once all the needed ports are included
