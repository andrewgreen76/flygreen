DHCP LEASE
===================================================================================

 . follows every DHCP discovery
 . A TIMED AGREEMENT on how long the client can stay connected with the given IP address (and other settings) 
 . all the necessary information aggregated during the DHCP discovery that the client can now use to configure
     itself at the network layer
 . the agreement is stored on both the DHCP client and the DHCP server
 . the server also records the clientâ€™s MAC-to-IP binding
 . after the expiration the client will have to negotiate a new lease to stay connected

 . the client can also terminate the lease upon disconnection
 . the IP address is then vacated and the server logs that internally
