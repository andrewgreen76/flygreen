======================================================
HIGH-PERFORMANCE MARKET VS. CAPACITY MARKET 
======================================================

For high performance :
 + low seek time
 + high rotational speed
 + fast transfer

For capacity :
 + pack as many bits as possible
 - slow operation
 . accounts for "cost per byte"
