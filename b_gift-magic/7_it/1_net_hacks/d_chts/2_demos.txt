> Run a Windows command prompt as administrator. 
> Regardless of what your current directory is in the console, simply type “telnet” and hit Enter to access Telnet. 
> Type ‘o’ to open a connection with a hostname/server and hit Enter. 
> Type “towel.blinkenlights.nl” for the hostname and hit Enter to open a connection with a server under that hostname. 

Watch the magic of ASCII unfold in Telnet as we are about to roll Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope in ASCII from the very start up until the very end!

================================= Telehack ================================
vvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvvv

> Run a Windows command prompt as administrator. 
> Type “telnet” and hit Enter. 
> Type ‘o’ to open a connection with a hostname and hit Enter. 
> Type “telehack.com” for the hostname and hit Enter. 

Welcome to Telehack! 

> After the dot (.) type “ping” and hit Enter. 
> Press Ctrl + T. 

> To get out of Telnet space press Ctrl + ].
