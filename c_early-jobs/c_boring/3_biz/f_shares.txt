
Q: "Consider this: "MicroProse had just gone public, and Bill Stealey needed a product for Summer 1992 or the
    stock would take a hit." One sentence: What does it mean for the "stock to take a hit"?"

A: "It means the company’s stock price would likely drop because investors would react negatively to the lack
    of a timely product release."


"
A STOCK PRICE is the current market value of one share of a company’s stock, determined by what buyers and sellers are willing to pay.
"


"
A STOCK SHARE is a unit of ownership in a company that represents a claim on a portion of its assets and earnings.
"


"
A share of stock is not a universal constant; its value and rights depend on company-specific factors such as total shares issued, ownership structure, and market conditions.
"


"
A falling stock price can hurt the company by reducing investor confidence, making it harder to raise capital, and potentially triggering leadership or strategic pressure.
"
