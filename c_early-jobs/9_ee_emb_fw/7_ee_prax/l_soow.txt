An SOOW cable is a portable, flexible power cord with a thermoset rubber jacket, rated for 600 volts, designed for heavy-duty industrial use, offering resistance to oil, water, and weather, commonly used in equipment like motors or temporary power setups.

"
SOOW stands for Service Cord, Oil-resistant insulation and jacket, Oil-resistant insulation, and Weather/Water-resistant, indicating a flexible, durable power cable designed for industrial use with resistance to oil, water, and harsh environmental conditions.
