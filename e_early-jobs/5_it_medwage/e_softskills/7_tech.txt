
===================================================================================
MISCELLANEOUS COMMUNICATION TIPS 
===================================================================================

An app "isn't working" :
 . blank display
 . the app is not responding to any user actions


Cc :
 . a.k.a. carbon copy
 . shows all recipients
Bcc :
 . a.k.a. blind carbon copy
 . hides all recipients
 . the idea is NOT TO DISCLOSE ADDESSES of others
