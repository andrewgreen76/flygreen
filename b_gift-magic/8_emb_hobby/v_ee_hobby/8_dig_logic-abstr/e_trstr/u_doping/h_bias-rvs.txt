===================================================================================

   [---black (anode)---|slv.strip (cathode)]
   p-type		       n-type
 . [-BAT+] ---(.- on wire)--- [-BAT+]	// conventional current model
 . [-BAT+] ---(.- on wire)--- [-BAT+]	// electron flow model
 . Lo.voltage/GND -------- Hi.voltage
 . little to no current
