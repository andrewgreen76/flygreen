
===================================================================================
THE OSI MODEL
===================================================================================

 . everything in that model is defined without any ambiguities
 => used in academia and by network certification organizations


The OSI model has seven layers :
 |
 . application :
    . in the OSI model it merely deals with managing data processed at the presentation layer and feeds it to
      applications 
 . presentation :
    . this implementation is a part of the OS 
    . for formatting , translation , compression , and encryption 
 . session
    . this implementation is a part of the OS 
    . takes the unencapsulated payload - application data - from the TCP segments' and feeds it to the
      presentation layer 
    . optimizes the workflow between the applications and the transport layer implementations
 . transport
 . network
 . data link
 . physical
