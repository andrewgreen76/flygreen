BCDEHLA : 
========================================================================================

"
The instruction set architecture featuring the instructions BCDEHLA was introduced with the Intel 8085 microprocessor, not the 8008. The 8085 microprocessor was released by Intel in 1976 as an improved version of the 8080 microprocessor. It featured enhancements over its predecessor, including additional instructions and improved performance.
