=========================================================================================
HANDLING LSE's
=========================================================================================

Solution : REDUNDANCY AND RECONSTRUCTION 
 . (in RAID) copying from the twin disk
 . (in RAID 4/5) using the parity disk or rotating parity
 - limited redundancy + one LSE = poor reconstruction
    + solution : more redundancy (second parity disk)
    - costs more
    + logging in LFS can make up for that
