
========================================================================================
SYSTEM MANAGEMENT MODE 
========================================================================================

 . system management capabilities are handed over to the kernel

 . "A modern system enters System Management Mode (SMM) after the initial boot process,
      typically triggered by specific hardware events or interrupts, allowing the processor
      to execute firmware-level tasks like power management and system control without
      interference from the operating system."

Programs that can be run : 
 . firmware (BIOS) routines 
 . certain vendors/manufacturers have the license to write init routines with certain
     system configurations
