
===================================================================================
SERVER RACK ASSEMBLY :
===================================================================================

Air-blocking boxes :
 . "Air-blocking boxes, or blanking panels, are used in server racks to prevent hot air recirculation
    and improve cooling efficiency by directing airflow through equipment, reducing energy costs
    and preventing overheating."


Switch rail kit : 
 . "... a set of mounting hardware, typically adjustable metal rails, designed to securely install
    a network switch or other equipment into a rack, allowing for easy sliding access and proper
    alignment within the rack structure."


BBU :
 . Battery Backup Unit
 . "... a device that provides temporary power to critical server components during power outages,
    ensuring data integrity and system stability until primary power is restored or a safe shutdown
    is completed."
