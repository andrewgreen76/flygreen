
===================================================================================
BOOTING INTO AN OS WITH WoL 
===================================================================================

Wake-on-LAN (WoL) is NOT A BOOTING METHOD.
 . the target device is to have a NIC with WoL capability
 . WoL is to be enabled in BIOS 
 . the target device is to be triggered to wake up upon receiving a signal over the WoL network
