DEVICES AND DRIVES ON WINDOWS
===================================================================================

 . devmgmt.msc : Device Manager console
 . Plug-and-Play (PnP) manager: recognizes and categorizes newly connected devices 
 . the corresponding driver for the device is located and installed 
 . every device has a hardware ID issued by the OEM (vendor)


Device driver installation - basics:
 . OS uses the ID to find the appropriate device driver:
   (a) going off of the installation disk
   (b) searching in the local list of common drivers
