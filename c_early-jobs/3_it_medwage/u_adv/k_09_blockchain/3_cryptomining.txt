Cryptomining is practiced by individuals, companies, and mining pools that run specialized hardware or software to
  support blockchain networks.
"


"
Cryptomining is the process of using computational power to validate transactions and add new blocks to a blockchain
  in exchange for rewards.
"


"
The rewards typically include newly created cryptocurrency coins and transaction fees paid by users.
"


"
Blockchain was first implemented in 2009 as the underlying technology for Bitcoin.
