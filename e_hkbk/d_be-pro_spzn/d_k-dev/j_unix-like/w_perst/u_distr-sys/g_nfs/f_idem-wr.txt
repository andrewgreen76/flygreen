HOW IS ***WRITE*** IDEMPOTENT : 
========================================================================================

Retrying WRITE : 
  |
  . first listened WRITE takes effect ; subsequent ones will not change the result 
  . actually functions as overwrite (of the same block , same piece of a file) 
  . WRITE is a bitwise operation => logical operation => idempotent

------------------------------------------------------------
------------------------------------------------------------
Example :
 . input    : 10100110
 . write in : 00001110

		  INPUT <---- OP ------> NEW -->
		10100110 & 00000000 | 00001110 = 00001110
		00001110 & 00000000 | 00001110 = 00001110
		00001110 & 00000000 | 00001110 = 00001110
			   .	      	 .
			   .		 .
			   .		 .
------------------------------------------------------------
------------------------------------------------------------

=> Target data is not necessarily unactionable.
