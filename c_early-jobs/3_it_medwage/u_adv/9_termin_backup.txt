
Q: "What does it mean for data to be encrypted at rest?"
|
A: "It means the data is stored in encrypted form on disk so it cannot be read without the decryption key."


Q: What is post-mortem?
|
A: The practice of documenting what went wrong and what steps can be taken to make sure the incident will never
     happen again.


Q: NAS is ...?
|
A: . "NAS stands for **Network Attached Storage**"
   . "A NAS device is a dedicated network-connected storage unit that provides shared file access to multiple
        users or systems."
