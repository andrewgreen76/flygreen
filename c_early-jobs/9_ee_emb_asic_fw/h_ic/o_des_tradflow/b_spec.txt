PLAN (SYSTEM SPECIFICATION)
===================================================================================

 . Define the chip's purpose and functionality
 . Identify the target application (e.g., smartphone, laptop, server)
 . Determine the chip's performance requirements:
    . Speed (clock frequency)
    . Power consumption
    . Area (size)
    . Interfaces (e.g., USB, HDMI)
 . Develop a written specification outlining the chip's requirements
